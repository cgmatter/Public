Common mistakes:

  • You must have ARM architecture (any of the M-series chips)
  • Blender must be downloaded from Blender.org (not elsewhere) and not portable
  • Your privileges are limited (give Blender/Terminal elevated priviledges)
  • You have conflicting ffmpeg, colmap, or rembg brew/pipx installations (clear these and reinstall)
  • You must refresh Blender after installation
